NDA
• The National Defence Academy (NDA) is a premier military training institute in India where cadets of the Army, Navy, and Air Force train together. Located in Pune, it develops leadership, discipline, and physical fitness. NDA prepares young candidates to become officers in the Indian Armed Forces. It is known for its tough training and prestigious reputation.
CDS
• The Combined Defence Services Examination (CDS) is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission twice a year. It is for graduates who want to join the Indian Army, Indian Navy, and Indian Air Force as officers. The selection process includes a written exam followed by an SSB interview and medical test.
AFCAT
• The Air Force Common Admission Test (AFCAT) is conducted by the Indian Air Force to recruit officers in Flying, Technical, and Ground Duty branches. It is held twice a year for graduates who want to join the Air Force as commissioned officers. The selection process includes a written exam, AFSB interview, and medical test.
